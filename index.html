Management of Budgetary Cases

The project was carried out in collaboration with Kelvin Garcia and developed for the accounting office "AYM Consultores". It involved the creation of a comprehensive system in C# using Entity Framework, Linq, and SQL Server, applying a layered architecture. The objective was to enable efficient tracking of administrative budgetary audits for schools, from their creation to approval and final archiving. The implemented solution optimized processes, providing greater control and efficiency in case management.
